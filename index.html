Mechanical engineer

After having worked as a trained car mechanic for 6 months, I wanted to go further. To not just fix the cars but build them (to be honest, I was not a gifted mechanic either). During university I started as soon as possible to gain real life experience. I joined the Formula Student team Delta Racing Mannheim e.V. and developed a new suspension for the electric and the combustion car.
My first job after graduation was at Pankl APC (now Pankl Turbosystems ) where I was part of the Design team for multiple high performance, electrically assisted racecar applications.
After two and a half years, I joined Trelleborg Sealing Solutions in Stuttgart where I designed prototype designs of high performance sealings for our national and international costumers.

  • Date: 2010 - 2019

  • Employers:
  • Delta-Racing Mannheim (Student)

Programmer

Having had an interest in programming since my C++ lectures in University, I decided that now is the time to make this my new carreer. I am learning Html, CSS and JS on freecodecamp.org to learn how to create an online presence to present and market my skills.
My main focus however is machine learning. After having finished my thesis in MatLab, I already have experiences with the language. I have recently finished Andrew Ng's Stanford Course in machine learning (to be found on Coursera ) and further my knowledge by actively learning Python to be able to better compete on Kaggle.
